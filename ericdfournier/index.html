I'm glad you made it. My name is Eric. I am a scientist. I study the earth's capability to support human life. I chose to do this partly because I am a human - who is alive - and partly because this capability appears to be in decline. This choice seemed logical enough.

Research

For the last few years I have done a considerable amount of work in the area of corridor location planning. This basically just involves devising techniques to rapidly deduce the shortest path between two points positioned within a space characterized by multiple and continuously non-uniform costs. The specific approach that I am developing to solve this problem is pretty cool in that it harnesses the power of random walks. These are interesting mathematical processes that show up in a surprisingly large number of contexts.
