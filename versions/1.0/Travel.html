Travel Destinations

  • Tanzania & Mozambique


     

    In 2008 I travelled to East Africa as part of a School for International Training field research program in coastal ecology. This was a tremendous experience for me and I cherish the memory of my time there.

  • Namibia & South Africa


     

    In 2010 Tess and I travelled to South Africa to tour Namibia and attend the World Cup in South Africa. The raw natural beauty of the desolate landscapes which we saw cannot be overstated.

  • Panama & Costa Rica


     

    In 2007 some college friends and I went on a surf trip to Costa Rica. Later, in 2013, I returned to Central America with some friends from graduate school for to surf in Bocas del Toro, Panama.

  • Germany & Austria


     

    In 2008 some friends from high school and I travelled to Europe for the World Cup in Germany. While there, we were fortunate enough to watch three games in the stadiums and saw a great deal of the country by train. It was fantastic.

  • Brazil


     

    In 2007 I went down to South America to visit my good friend Pedro who lives in Rio De Janeiro. We had more fun than should be legal and I owe him a great debt for his hospitality. I'm still waiting for Pedro to come visit me in Santa Barbara so that I can return the favor.

Travel Philosophy

When it comes to travel, I really enjoy going to places that are as far off the beaten path as possible. This usually leads to places like deserts and islands that can be quite difficult to get to and expensive to navigate.

 

Regardless, if you have an idea for a place that you would like to go, and for whatever reason, you would like me to come with, please drop me a line.
